About Me

Hello and welcome! Whether you're navigating big transitions, relationship challenges, or emotional ups and downs, therapy can be a powerful space to pause, reflect, and grow. Together, we'll explore your strengths, clarify your goals, and move toward healing and authenticity.

I'm a Russian-English speaking licensed psychotherapist with over 7 years of clinical experience. Having personally navigated cultural transitions while raising two young children and managing a full professional life, I understand how hard it can be to feel centered. I specialize in treating anxiety, depression, communication and relationship issues.

I am using a warm, collaborative, and integrative approach tailored to your needs and will be honored to support you in your journey toward greater self-awareness and well-being. If you're ready to start feeling more grounded, connected, and empowered, I'm here to help.
