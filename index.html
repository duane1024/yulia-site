Welcome

A grounded space to pause, reflect, and grow.

Whether you are navigating big transitions, relationship challenges, or emotional ups and downs, therapy can be a powerful place to reconnect with yourself. Together, we will explore your strengths, clarify your goals, and move toward healing and authenticity.

I am a Russian-English speaking licensed psychotherapist with over 8 years of clinical experience. Having personally navigated cultural transitions while raising two young children and managing a full professional life, I understand how hard it can be to feel centered.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and integrative. I specialize in anxiety, depression, communication and relationship issues, coping skills, self-esteem, life transitions, parenting, and stress.

Approach

A warm and practical approach focused on clarity and emotional insight.

Therapy that combines emotional insight with practical tools for everyday life.

01

Understand what is happening

We slow down the pressure, identify what is underneath the surface, and make sense of the thoughts, emotions, and relationship patterns that keep repeating.

02

Build tools you can use

I use an integrative, pragmatic approach anchored in mindfulness, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and Acceptance Committment Therapy (ACT).

03

Move forward with support

We clarify goals, notice progress, and adjust the work so therapy remains responsive to your life rather than a one-size-fits-all process.
